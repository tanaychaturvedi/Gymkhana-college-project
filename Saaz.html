Saaz CLub

Saaz is Musical club.

Music connects the brain to the heart. Music heals the body. If You find music and singing your hobby or passion then this club is for you. Many club members are of professional level ,they are extremely skilled, and always ready to teach new enthusiastics. We Have our special Music Room in Hall 3 for practicing. This room is equippted with a lot of musical instruments. We conduct weekly practice sessions and arrange various competitions among new members. You will definitely enjoy the group no matter you are music lover or not, this club will grow your interest.

Club equipments:-

  1. All musical instruments

  2. Fully electric Music hall

  3. High Quality Speakers

  4. Guitar, Tabla, Piano, etc.

Benefits:-

  1. Access to instruments any time you want

  2. A life time memory

  3. Get to know about music and singing

  4. Interacting with singers

  5. Competitions and trips

  6. Awards and many certificate
